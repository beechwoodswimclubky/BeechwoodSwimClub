## Team information page

### We are the Beechwood Barracudas!

The Beechwood Barracuda Swim and Dive Team registration is now open for the 2026 season on the [Beechwood Swim & Dive Team Website](https://www.gomotionapp.com/team/recnkbscky/page/home).

**Why Join the Barracudas Swim and Dive Team?**

Joining the Barracudas is more than just participating in a sport; it's about making lifelong friends, boosting confidence, and creating lasting summer memories. Our team competes in the Northern Kentucky Swim League (NKSL), providing a friendly yet competitive environment for all skill levels.

**Key Dates and Events**

1. **Information Night:** Thursday, May 28th at 7pm at the Lower Deck Shelter. Meet the coaches, learn about the season, and get all your questions answered. Athletes are welcome too!
2. **Practices Begin:** Monday, June 1st for the Swim/Dive Team; Tuesday, June 2nd for Little Cudas

**Participation Requirements**

All pool members in good standing are eligible to join. Swimmers must be able to swim one lap of freestyle and backstroke unassisted. If not, they can join our Little Cuda Swim Team, where they'll receive personalized coaching to improve their skills.

Parents are required to volunteer for at least 4 sessions during the season for 1 athlete and 5 sessions for 2+ athletes. Most swim meet 'sessions' involve volunteering for 1/2 of a meet. Sign up for jobs will be posted on the Team Unify site under 'Events & Meets' in late May and information on volunteer positions can be found in the 'Parents Info' section of our website.

Additional information about the season can be found on our website, including the post-season schedule, team suits, spiritwear, coach info and registration! Check it out - [Beechwood Swim & Dive Team Website](https://www.gomotionapp.com/team/recnkbscky/page/home).

The Beechwood Barracudas are a summer recreational swim and dive team that is a member of the Northern Kentucky Swim League.  The season begins in early June and typically ends in late July.  Practices are held Monday through Thursday mornings.  Diving meets are on Tuesday evenings and Swimming meets are on Thursday evenings.

The main goal of the Beechwood Barracudas is to provide a fun environment where the children can make new friends while becoming stronger swimmers and graceful divers.  All skill levels are welcome.  To be on the swim team, your child should be able to swim 1 length of the pool freestyle and 1 length backstroke.  If a swimmer cannot swim the length of the pool, they can start with the Little Cudas, which will work on teaching the fundamentals of competitive swimming and building up endurance.
